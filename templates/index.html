About ARV

Associative Remote Viewing (ARV) is an experimental technique that attempts to predict future outcomes by viewing associated targets. This platform facilitates collaborative ARV research with:

  • Cryptographic commit-reveal schemes
  • Real-time collaborative descriptors
  • Multi-judge scoring systems
  • Statistical aggregation algorithms
  • Optional AI-powered embeddings
